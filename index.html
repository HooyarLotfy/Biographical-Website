About Me, Hooyar Lotfy

Hooyar Lotfy 🌍 Born in 2008, I was an energetic and curious child, always eager to understand my purpose in life.

At 8, I dove into programming with Visual Basic (even though it was already outdated 💻). By 9, I had mastered WordPress and started creating websites for family businesses. 🖥️

At 10, I had a big realization: I wanted to reach the top of the world through technology! 🔝 I focused on learning math basics, solving IQ problems 🧠, and sharpening my mind to tackle programming challenges. I began learning C++, but realized I still needed more mental strength. Despite that, I could build simple apps like calculators and daily tools. 🛠️

At 13.5, a family friend introduced me to game development using Unreal Engine 5 (UE5) 🎮. I saved up and bought a laptop 💻, diving into the world of game development. After 2.5 years of experience—1.5 years of building projects and 1 year of learning—I felt confident in my skills and decided I had enough experience. 🎯

In the same year, my family and I moved to Istanbul 🌆. Here, I found more opportunities to reach my goals, and I am determined to achieve everything I aspire to 🚀. This vibrant city offers a wealth of resources and connections that will help me on my journey to success 🌟.

Now, in January 2025, at 16, I’m shifting focus to web development 🌐, mastering the skills before pursuing software development during my bachelor’s degree 🎓. Over the next 6 years, I plan to master both web and software dev, while also publishing solo-developed game projects! 🚀

My goal? To reach the top, whether it's through wealth 💰, power 💪, or becoming an idol 🌟. That’s who I am: driven, ambitious, and focused on greatness. 🙌
